Product Overview

KrakenSDR is a revolutionary 5-Channel Coherent SDR system designed for advanced applications including radio direction finding (RDF) and passive radar. Built on five custom RTL-SDR circuits all synchronized to a single local oscillator, it provides unprecedented phase coherence for precision bearing measurements and target tracking. Housed in a rugged CNC-milled aluminum enclosure that doubles as an efficient heat sink, the KrakenSDR delivers professional-grade thermal stability critical for maintaining phase coherence across all channels.
