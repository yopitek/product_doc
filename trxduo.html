Product Overview

Professional dual-channel SDR transceiver platform featuring Xilinx Zynq7010 SoC with dual-core ARM Cortex-A9 processor. Equipped with dual 16-bit LTC2208 ADCs sampling at 125MS/s for high dynamic range reception from 10kHz to 60MHz. Includes 14-bit DAC for dual-channel transmission. Fully compatible with Red Pitaya SDR software ecosystem including HDSDR, SDR#, PowerSDR, and SDR Console. High sensitivity, excellent dynamic range, and convenient remote deployment make it ideal for HF/VHF communications research, spectrum monitoring, and SDR development.
