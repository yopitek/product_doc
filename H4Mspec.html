Product Overview

The H4M Mayhem is a professional-grade portable Software Defined Radio (SDR) platform combining the HackRF One transceiver with the Mayhem Signature-Edition PortaPack. Designed for standalone operation, it delivers comprehensive signal analysis and transmission capabilities without requiring a computer, making it ideal for field operations, research, and educational applications.
