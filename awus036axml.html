Important Notes

  • USB 3.0 Required: USB 3.0 port required for maximum throughput. Backward compatible with USB 2.0 (up to 480 Mbps).
  • WiFi 6E Router: To utilize 6 GHz band and achieve maximum speeds, a WiFi 6E compatible router is required.
  • Antenna Upgrade: RP-SMA connectors allow for optional higher-gain antenna upgrades for extended range.
  • Driver Installation: Always download the latest drivers from the official ALFA Network website for optimal performance.
