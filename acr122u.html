ACS ACR122U

13.56MHz USB NFC Reader/Writer

Professional contactless smart card reader/writer designed for secure identity verification, access control, and NFC applications across multiple platforms.

ISO 14443 Compliant NFC Compatible Plug & Play Up to 424 kbps

Key Features

Advanced capabilities for professional NFC applications

Contactless Technology

Built-in antenna with reading distance up to 50mm, supporting all major contactless card types and NFC standards.

High-Speed Performance

Read/write speeds up to 424 kbps with USB full speed support up to 12 Mbps for efficient data transfer.

Universal Compatibility

CCID and PC/SC compliant, ensuring plug-and-play functionality across Windows, Mac, Linux, and Android.

Multi-Protocol Support

Supports ISO 14443 Type A & B, MIFARE, FeliCa, and all four types of NFC tags for maximum flexibility.

Anti-Collision Feature

Built-in anti-collision ensures only one card is accessed at a time, preventing interference from multiple tags.

Compact Design

Sleek white PC enclosure measuring just 90×65×12.8mm and weighing only 100g, perfect for any workspace.
