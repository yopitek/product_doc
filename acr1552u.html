ISO 15693 Support

Comprehensive support for ISO 15693 cards including SRI, SRIX, CTS, Innovatron, and Picopass technologies.

SAM Security

Built-in Secure Access Module slot for key diversification and mutual authentication providing enhanced security.

FIDO Authentication

Support for FIDO protocols with PocketKey and YubiKey compatibility for secure passwordless authentication.

Firmware Upgradeable

In-field firmware upgrade capability via USB ensuring long-term adaptability to evolving technologies.

Extended APDU

Support for extended APDU commands enabling complex operations and enhanced application flexibility.

Smart Indicators

Programmable bi-color LED and buzzer for customizable visual and audio feedback during operations.

Plug & Play

CCID and PC/SC compliant for instant compatibility across platforms with no special driver installation required.
