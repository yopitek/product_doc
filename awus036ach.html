Important Information

  • USB 3.0 Recommended: A USB 3.0 port is recommended for optimal performance. The adapter is backward compatible with USB 2.0 but speeds will be limited.
  • Extended Range: Internal power amplifier and high-gain antennas provide up to 3× the range of standard laptop WiFi cards.
  • Antenna Upgrades: RP-SMA female connectors allow for higher-gain antenna upgrades to further extend wireless coverage and range.
  • Driver Installation: Always download the latest drivers from the official ALFA Network website for optimal performance, stability, and security.
