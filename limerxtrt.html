Product Overview

The LimeSDR XTRX is a high-performance software-defined radio board in a compact Mini PCIe form factor, designed for embedding in larger systems and building MIMO configurations from 2Tx2R to 32Tx32R. Based on the Fairwaves XTRX Rev 5 with significant refinements, it features the powerful Lime Microsystems LMS7002M RF transceiver and AMD Artix 7 FPGA, delivering exceptional flexibility for logic-intensive digital and RF designs across various air interfaces.

Important Notes
  • RF Antennas: U.FL antennas and RF cables are required and sold separately
  • Power Requirements: Can be powered via USB 2.0 (5V) or Mini PCIe slot (3.3V)
  • TX Power: Maximum transmit power of 10 dBm is frequency-dependent; check specifications for target band
  • Compliance: Users are responsible for compliance with local RF regulations and licensing requirements
  • FPGA Development: Custom FPGA development requires Xilinx Vivado Design Suite
