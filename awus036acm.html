Important Information

  • USB 3.0 Recommended: A USB 3.0 port is recommended for maximum throughput performance. The adapter is backward compatible with USB 2.0.
  • Enhanced Range: External 5 dBi antennas provide superior range and wall penetration compared to internal antenna designs.
  • Antenna Upgrade: RP-SMA connectors allow for optional higher-gain antenna upgrades to further extend wireless range.
  • Driver Installation: Always download the latest drivers from the official ALFA Network website for optimal performance and compatibility.
