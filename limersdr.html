Product Overview

LimeSDR is an open source, apps-enabled software defined radio platform that can be used to support just about any type of wireless communication standard. This versatile platform handles UMTS, LTE, GSM, LoRa, Bluetooth, Zigbee, RFID, and Digital Broadcasting communications, making it ideal for a wide range of applications from radio astronomy and RADAR to cellular basestations, IoT gateways, HAM radio, drone control, test/measurement, and aerospace applications.

Key Advantages
Wide Coverage: 100 kHz to 3.8 GHz frequency range
High Bandwidth: 61.44 MHz RF bandwidth for wideband signals
Full MIMO: 2×2 configuration with independent TX/RX chains
USB 3.0: High-speed data transfer for real-time processing

FPGA Power: Flexible signal processing capabilities
Multi-Standard: Support for all major wireless protocols
Important Notes
  • RF Antennas: U.FL antennas and RF cables are required and sold separately
  • Power Supply: Can be powered via Micro USB or optional external power supply
  • TX Power: Maximum output power of 10 dBm (CW); actual power varies by frequency and configuration
  • Compliance: Users are responsible for compliance with local RF regulations and licensing requirements
  • Operating System: Best supported on Linux-based systems; Windows and macOS drivers also available
  • Development: FPGA development requires Altera Quartus II or compatible toolchain
