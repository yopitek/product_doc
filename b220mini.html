Product Overview

Professional SDR development board based on USRP B210 design with AD9361 RF transceiver. Featuring full-duplex 2TX/2RX MIMO capability, 70MHz to 6GHz frequency coverage, and 56MHz instantaneous bandwidth. Fully compatible with USRP Hardware Driver (UHD), GNU Radio, and OpenAirInterface (OAI). Ideal for wireless communications research, spectrum analysis, and 4G/5G protocol development.
