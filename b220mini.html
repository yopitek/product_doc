Product Overview

Professional SDR development board with full USRP B210 compatibility. Features AD9361 RF transceiver with 2TX/2RX MIMO, Xilinx Artix7 FPGA (7A100T/7A200T), and USB 3.0 Type-C interface. Supports external clock taming, PPS/10M reference frequency synchronization, and phase synchronization. Enhanced with ADI PA using 5V power supply for improved efficiency. Perfect for 4G/5G research, MIMO systems, and wireless protocol development.
