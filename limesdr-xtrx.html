Product Overview

About LimeSDR XTRX

The LimeSDR XTRX is a high-performance software-defined radio (SDR) board designed in a compact Mini PCIe form factor, optimized for embedding into larger systems. It represents a significant evolution from the Fairwaves XTRX Rev 5, featuring comprehensive refinements and extensive software support.

This platform is ideal for logic-intensive digital and RF design applications requiring embedded SDR capabilities in compact form factors, making it perfect for both research and commercial deployments.

Key Highlights

  • Scalable MIMO systems from 2×2 up to 32×32 configurations
  • Open-source design with publicly available schematics and FPGA gateware
  • Compatible with various digital processors (ASICs, GPPs, GPUs)
  • Supports both narrowband and broadband air interfaces
