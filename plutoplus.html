2T2R Zynq7010 FPGA GigE USB OTG MicroSD
Product Overview

Professional SDR platform based on ADALM-PLUTO design with significant improvements. Features 2TX/2RX capability, Zynq7010 FPGA processing, Gigabit Ethernet connectivity, and MicroSD boot support. Equipped with AD9363 transceiver (upgradable to AD9361/AD9364) covering 70MHz to 6GHz spectrum with high-precision VCTCXO reference clock.
